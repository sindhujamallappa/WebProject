         About and Services

                 The Government of India provides scholarships to all those students who are not able to finance their education. For this purpose,the government has launched a National Scholarship Portal.
    Through the National scholarship portal, the students can apply for various scholarship schemes offered by the central and state government. This portal has been launched by the Ministrry of Electronincs and Information tecnology,
    Government of India. Through this portalthe beneficiaries can even check their application status. If you want to get a scholarshipoffered by the government then you have to applu online on the National Scholarship portal by following
    the procedure. fresh applications are also being accepted fron August 2021, The students are not rquired to visit the government offices in order to apply the scholarships . they just have togo to the official website to apply for a scholarship.

                  To encourage educatio the government provides scholarships to all the eligible students. The scholarship is provided every year. For the year 2021-2022 the last date to apply for a scholarship has been extended by the government. The higher education director of the state has issued a notification to all the schools, colleges, universities. In this notification, he has asked all the educatonal institutions to ensure that all the eligible students apply for a scholarship before last date. All the school students for level 1.1 and level 1.2 can apply for scholarship till 31st December 2021. The last date of the first phase verification is 31st January 2022 and the last date of 2nd phase verificatio is 28th February 2022. all the minority syudents who wish to apply for the scholarship on pre metric, post matriculation, and merit basis will have toapply before 31st december 2021.
